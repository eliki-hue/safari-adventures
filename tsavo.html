Tsavo

Tsavo East is one of the oldest parks. Moreover it is the largest national park in Kenya, and together with Tsavo West covers just over 20,000 square kilometres, or 4% of Kenya.
Furthermore also it has more biodiversity than any other park globally, as it marks the meeting point between northern and southern wildlife.
It is approximately 9 times bigger than the Maasai Mara and is well known for its mane-less lions, big herds of red dust-covered elephants, and cheetah. We have seen here cheetah hunting, lions and many other animals.
